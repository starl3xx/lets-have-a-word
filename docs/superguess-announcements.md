# Superguess Announcements

All announcements are posted by @letshaveaword on Farcaster (and cross-posted to Twitter/X when enabled). Push notifications go to all users who have notifications enabled.

---

## Superguess Started

**Farcaster cast:**
```
🔴 SUPERGUESS activated — @username has 25 guesses and 10 minutes. All eyes on them. 👀

Watch live 👉 letshaveaword.fun
```

**Push notification:**
```
Title: 🔴 SUPERGUESS purchased
Body:  @username is making a Superguess — 25 guesses with 10 minutes on the clock. Watch live!
```

## Superguess Failed (exhausted or expired)

```
🔴 Superguess over — @username used 25/25 guesses but didn’t find the secret word.

Normal play resumes now! 👉 letshaveaword.fun
```

**Farcaster cast (with bonus/burn words found):**
```
🔴 Superguess over — @username used 25/25 guesses but didn’t find the secret word.

But they did find 1 bonus word and 2 burn words along the way 🕵️‍♂️

Normal play resumes now! 👉 letshaveaword.fun
```
